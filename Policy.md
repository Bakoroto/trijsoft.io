# Privacy Policy for Markdown Editor

**Effective Date: July 7, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Markdown Editor** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### A. Personal Data
* **No Direct Collection:** We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number directly within our services.
* **Google Drive Sync (User-Authorized):** If you opt to use the **Google Drive Sync** feature, the App will request authorization to access your Google Drive space via Google OAuth. 
  * The App only accesses the files you explicitly choose to save, sync, or open using the App.
  * Your Google credentials and authentication tokens are stored securely on your device's local storage and are never sent to our servers.
  * We do not read, store, or share your Google account details or files on any third-party servers.

### B. Local Data Storage
* All of your notes, draft files, and workspace preferences are stored locally on your device using an offline SQLite database (Room Database). 
* None of your document content is uploaded, shared, or processed by us or any external party, except when you explicitly initiate a manual export (e.g., PDF Export) or use the Google Drive Sync feature to sync files to your own personal Google Drive account.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services & SDKs

The App integrates certain third-party SDKs to provide essential features and monetize the application. These third parties may collect information used to identify you.

### A. Google AdMob (Advertising)
We display banner advertisements in the App using Google AdMob to keep the application free to use.
* Google AdMob may collect and process telemetry data, device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), IP addresses, and generalized location data to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements.
* You can manage ad personalization or opt out of personalized tracking through your Android device settings (**Settings > Google > Ads**).
* For more details, please review [Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### B. Google Play Services / Google Drive API
* Used to facilitate Google sign-in and file syncing to your Google Drive account.
* For more details, please review the [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 3. Device Permissions

To deliver all requested functionalities, the App may request the following system permissions on your Android device:

* **Internet Access (`android.permission.INTERNET`):** Required to fetch advertisements from AdMob, download Markdown guides, and perform Google Drive backups/syncs.
* **Network State (`android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`):** Used to check if an active connection is available before attempting to sync files or load ads.
* **Storage / Media Access (Optional):** Required to read/write Markdown text files from/to local device storage and export notes as PDF documents.

You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time through your device's application settings.

## 4. Data Security

We value your trust in using our App. All note processing and PDF rendering are performed locally on your device. We use industry-standard practices to protect any configuration tokens or local databases. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our records. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
